Ruffus Rescue, Inc.

ONLINE ADOPTION APPLICATION

To: THOSE WHO HAVE ADOPTED FROM RUFFUS RESCUE OR WANT TO ADOPT A PET

 

We want everyone to know that Ruffus Rescue is still in operation though on a smaller and different scale such as taking in and adopting out certain special case dogs. These include dogs from our previous adopters who have passed away, and some who are not able to care for their dogs due to a baby being born or some who are having to move to a place that won't accept their pets. We want to let you know that if you would care to donate to help us out it would be greatly appreciated. We are also assisting pet owners whose dogs need medical attention and food and they cannot afford the entire medical bill but want to keep their pet and we are assisting small rescue groups who are trying to stay afloat. We also help out by donating to cruelty cases of which there are many heartbreaking ones out there. We still depend on your donations to help these animals that can't help themselves. Should you decide to donate to Ruffus Rescue you will receive a thank you letter from us letting you know what your donation was used for and it will be used as a tax deduction as we are a 501(c)(3) tax exempt rescue group. Please make all checks out to Ruffus Rescue, Inc. To donate please send a check to:

We thank you for the 10 wonderful years of adopting dogs from us and appreciate all the cards and pictures you are sending. In those years, we have adopted out over 2,300 dogs to wonderful and caring homes and we are grateful that we can remain in rescue by helping dogs in need whether they be Ruffus Rescue dogs or others. We can only do this with your help. If there is a certain type of dog you are looking for please let us know and we can try to assist you in finding that dog.
