To: THOSE WONDERFUL PEOPLE WHO HAVE ADOPTED A DOG OR DOGS FROM RUFFUS RESCUE OR THOSE WHO WANT TO ADOPT A DOG FROM US!

 

It's Harriett Patterson, Director and Founder of Ruffus Rescue writing to let you know that we are back in operation!! Ruffus Rescue has added to it's original statement and we are now trying to assist dog owners who, due to unfortunate circumstances such as those who are unable to afford preventative medicine, spay/neuter costs dog food etc, but want to be able to keep their dogs in the family. We will weigh each case that is presented to us and choose the ones we are able to assist.

Some more exciting news is that Ruffus Rescue has resumed adoptions at our original site, PET SUPPLIES PLUS, located at: 2329 Cheshire Bridge Rd., Atlanta, GA 30324 from 12noon to 5pm. (please call the them at 404-325-8300 for weekend days of operation). Initially we will be there on Saturdays but may add Sundays after Easter weekend! We are limiting the number of dogs we take into our program and bring to our adoptions until we are able to find more foster homes, so please consider helping us out by fostering a dog! For information on fostering please email me at harriett.patterson@att.net or fill out and submit our ONLINE ADOPTION APPLICATION. You will be contacted within 24 hours of submitting an application.

Ruffus Rescue would greatly appreciate your help financially as well no matter the amount! Your contributions will enable us to carry out our mission of helping the dogs in need as well as assisting us in spaying/neutering, shots and fecal and heartworm tests for the limited number of dogs we are able to take into our rescue program.

Should you decide to choose Ruffus Rescue as your charity of choice to donate to, you can either send a check payable to Ruffus Rescue and mail it to the address below or you can go to our Paypal Account link below and donate with your credit card. Either way, you will receive a thank you letter for your taxes as we are a 501(c)(3) tax exempt dog rescue group which makes your contribution tax deductible.
